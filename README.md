## About Me

I work at the intersection of **artificial intelligence and offensive security**. I am an active **CTF player and pentester** with strong hands-on experience breaking and analyzing systems, and I apply the same attacker mindset when building AI-driven solutions.

My main focus is on **neural networks, computer vision, and applied machine learning**, with a growing interest in how AI can be used for **security automation, intrusion detection, adversarial machine learning, and attack simulation**.

I enjoy environments where I can both **build and break systems**, whether that is through AI competitions, hackathons, CTFs, or independent projects.
